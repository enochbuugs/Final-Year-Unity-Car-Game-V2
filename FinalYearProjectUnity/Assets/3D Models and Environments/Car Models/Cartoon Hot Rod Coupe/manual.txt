All possible variants for all the 3 modifications are collected in 18 prefabs. It is enough to draw the prefab in the chosen place to add a needed variant.
If required occlusion and emission maps can be minimized almost without degenaration of quality, just as the others. 
Yet the standard size of all texture maps for each model is 2048*2048 px.
These models are rendered for the wheel collider settings / customization.
